The items, apps, and services I use to manage my work and life.

**Goals**
1. Avoid ecosystems. Many people have been blocked off google, Apple, and lost access to a large part of their life. Instead, find find services that do one thing well. Easier to substitute them later on if they break or you find something better.
3. Avoid proprietary formats, maximize ease of migration
4. Store personal data in a synced folder that apps read from
5. Prefer things that don't need accounts
